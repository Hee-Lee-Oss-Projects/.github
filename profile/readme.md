# Elyos Projects

> *From the Greek **Eleos** (Ἔλεος) — the personification of compassion and mercy.*

**Elyos turns spare AI capacity into open-source good deeds.** A contributor pulls a
community-approved task, has **their own coding agent** do the work, and opens a PR — using
capacity they already pay for. It also runs **AI knowledge-work for good** (translation,
education, public-data analysis), not just coding software. Many AI Users are on subscription plans where
their credits expire daily or weekly if they go unused, rather than allowing this capability to go to waste Elyos
gives you the option to use your spare credits which may be about to expire for good-deed style
software projects and repositories. 


**What if you could do a good thing, what if you could do 100 good things, 1000 good things... Every week. 

**The intention of Elyos is to put this compute power to work for the good of mankind, rather than letting it expire.
